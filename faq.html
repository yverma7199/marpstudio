Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Booking & Appointments

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for regular appointments and 3-6 months in advance for wedding bookings. Popular dates get booked quickly, so early booking is advised.

Do you require a deposit for bookings?

+

Yes, we require a 25% deposit to secure your booking. For wedding packages, a 50% deposit is required. The remaining balance is due on the day of your appointment.

Services & Pricing

What's included in your bridal makeup package?

+

Our complete bridal package includes: trial session, full makeup application, hairstyling, touch-up kit, and ongoing support throughout your wedding day. We also include complementary skincare consultation.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

+

Yes! We offer destination wedding services across India and internationally. Travel costs are additional and quoted based on your location and requirements.

Products & Techniques

What brands of makeup do you use?

+

We use premium, professional-grade brands including MAC, Bobbi Brown, NARS, Charlotte Tilbury, and Huda Beauty. All products are sanitized and suitable for all skin types.

Do you offer airbrush makeup?

+

Yes, we specialize in airbrush makeup which provides flawless, long-lasting coverage that's perfect for photography and humid conditions.
