Cancellation Policy

Appointment Cancellations

  • Cancellations made 3 days or more before the appointment: Full refund
  • Cancellations made 2 -3 days before the appointment: 50% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the appointment: No refund
  • Emergency cancellations (with valid proof): Case-by-case consideration

Wedding & Event Bookings

  • Cancellation 30+ days before event: 80% refund
  • Cancellation 15-30 days before event: 50% refund
  • Cancellation 7-14 days before event: 25% refund
  • Cancellation less than 7 days before event: No refund
